Oreo Fluff

Description

This recipe offers a sweet conclusion to any meal. It's the perfect dessert or snack, and with just a few ingredients, it's extremely simple to whip up. The flavor strikes a balance between light and decadent.

Who doesn't love Oreos?

Ingredients

  • 1 (1-ounce) box instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 (8-ounce) container Cool Whip
  • 6 Oreos, crushed

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the pudding mix and milk for 2 minutes.
  2. Fold in the Cool Whip and Oreos.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until fluff has set, about 1 hour.
